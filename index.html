About GreenAI

We're transforming traditional agriculture through intelligent automation and data-driven greenhouse management.

Smart Solutions for Sustainable Growth

GreenAI was founded in 2023 with a mission to revolutionize greenhouse farming through artificial intelligence. Our proprietary systems monitor and adjust environmental conditions in real-time, optimizing plant growth while reducing resource consumption.

By integrating IoT sensors, computer vision, and machine learning algorithms, we create the perfect growing conditions for any crop, anywhere in the world. Our technology reduces water usage by up to 70% while increasing yields by 30-50% compared to traditional methods.
