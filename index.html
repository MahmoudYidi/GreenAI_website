Comprehensive AI Solutions

End-to-end greenhouse intelligence for modern glasshouse agriculture

Green Vision

Our AI-powered vision stack monitors every plant in real time. Detecting diseases early, tracking growth stages, counting fruits, identifying pests, and spotting nutrient deficiencies. From canopy analysis to yield prediction, we turn your glasshouse into a fully autonomous growing environment.

Green Climate

Our AI dynamically adjusts temperature, humidity, and CO₂ based on each crop's needs—responding to plant health, growth stage, and ambient weather conditions to create the ideal microclimate at every moment.

Green Resources

Machine learning precisely calculates each crop's water, nutrient, and resource needs—eliminating waste, preventing overuse, and ensuring efficient, sustainable growing in every corner of the glasshouse.
